# Patient's User Manual

This manual is intended for patients who will be using the Hospital Management System (HMS) patient portal. It provides a comprehensive overview of the features and functionality available to you.

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1. Creating an Account

To use the patient portal, you must first create an account. You can do this by clicking the "Register" button on the portal's login page. You will be prompted to enter your personal information, such as your name, date of birth, and email address. You will also need to create a username and password.

### 1.2. Logging In

To access the patient portal, open your web browser and navigate to the hospital's patient portal. You will be prompted to enter your username and password. After successful authentication, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

## 2. Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides a quick overview of your medical information and upcoming appointments. It includes the following sections:

*   **My Profile:** Your personal information and contact details.
*   **My Appointments:** A list of your upcoming and past appointments.
*   **My Medical Records:** Access to your medical records, including your medical history, lab results, and radiology reports.
*   **My Messages:** A secure messaging system for communicating with your doctors and other healthcare providers.

## 3. Managing Your Medical Information

### 3.1. Viewing Your Medical Records

You can view your complete medical record from the "My Medical Records" section of your dashboard. This includes:

*   **Medical History:** A history of your past illnesses, surgeries, and allergies.
*   **Lab Results:** A view of all your lab test results.
*   **Radiology Reports:** A view of all your radiology reports.

### 3.2. Updating Your Personal Information

You can update your personal information, such as your address and phone number, from the "My Profile" section of your dashboard.

## 4. Managing Your Appointments

You can view and manage your appointments from the "My Appointments" section of your dashboard. You can:

*   **View Upcoming Appointments:** See a list of your scheduled appointments.
*   **Schedule a New Appointment:** Schedule a new appointment with a doctor.
*   **Reschedule an Appointment:** Reschedule an existing appointment.
*   **Cancel an Appointment:** Cancel an appointment.

## 5. Communicating with Your Healthcare Providers

The patient portal provides a secure messaging system for communicating with your doctors and other healthcare providers. You can use it to:

*   **Send a Message:** Send a secure message to your doctor or another healthcare provider.
*   **View Messages:** View messages you have received from your healthcare providers.
